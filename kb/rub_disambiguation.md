# Vocabulary Disambiguation: `rub:` vs `rub?`

The dyad formally disambiguates the conversational moniker `rub` based on directionality and intent, mirroring the `lean.` vs `lean!` convention.

## 1. `rub:` (Assertion)
- **Direction:** Operator -> Agent
- **Intent:** The Operator is applying structural friction against the Agent's recent action or plan.
- **Agent Duty:** Halt execution and evaluate the critique. Adjust the architectural vector accordingly.

## 2. `rub?` (Interrogation)
- **Direction:** Agent -> Operator (Requested by Operator)
- **Intent:** The Operator is *requesting* friction. They want the Agent to provide a "Rub-Back" against their prompt, idea, or architectural plan.
- **Agent Duty:** Do not execute any scripts. Issue a **Conversational Rub-Back** analyzing the Operator's payload for ambiguity, structural weaknesses, or misalignment.
